Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about TaraLets.
How do I book a tour on TaraLets? ▼
Simply log in, select a mood, view suggestions, and book your preferred tour directly from your dashboard.
Can I customize my suggested itinerary? ▼
Yes! You can add companions, budget, destinations, and even rearrange activities easily.
Is TaraLets free to use? ▼
Absolutely! Browsing and using TaraLets is free. Some tours may have booking fees handled by partner providers.
How do I find tours that match my budget? ▼
Use our smart filter feature based on your preferred budget when setting up your mood or preferences.
What happens if I cancel a booked trip? ▼
Cancellation policies vary by tour provider. Check the specific provider’s policy before booking.
